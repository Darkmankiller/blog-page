About Firewall101

Our mission is to democratize cybersecurity knowledge for everyone.

Our Story

Firewall101 was founded in 2023 by Goutham Krishnan, a BSc Cyber Forensic graduate passionate about cybersecurity education.

We believe that cybersecurity shouldn't be obscure or intimidating. By presenting complex security concepts in accessible ways, we aim to help individuals and businesses protect themselves in an increasingly digital world.

What started as a small collection of guides has grown into a comprehensive resource trusted by cybersecurity enthusiasts and professionals alike.

About the Founder

Goutham Krishnan

Founder & Lead Security Researcher

BSc in Cyber Forensics with expertise in digital investigation techniques and cybersecurity best practices. Passionate about making cybersecurity knowledge accessible to everyone and helping organizations protect their digital assets.

Our Core Values

Accessibility

We believe security knowledge should be available to everyone, regardless of technical background.

Practicality

Our content focuses on actionable steps you can implement immediately to improve your security posture.

Ethics

We promote responsible security practices and never share information that could be used maliciously.

Innovation

As threats evolve, so do we. We're committed to exploring cutting-edge security techniques and technologies.
